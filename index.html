Dr. Adil Aziz Khan is a Consultant Neurosurgeon with over a decade of clinical experience in diagnosing and treating complex disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system. He specializes in the management of headache disorders, seizures, fits, loss of consciousness, traumatic brain injuries, and spinal conditions including backache and disc disease. With advanced postgraduate training in neurosurgery, Dr. Adil Aziz Khan is committed to providing evidence-based, patient-focused care using modern neurosurgical principles in a safe and compassionate clinical environment.

Years of Excellence

Professional Profile

Compassionate Care & Clinical Excellence

Dr. Adil Aziz Khan is a highly trained FCPS-qualified Neurosurgeon with years of experience in both emergency and elective neurosurgical care. He completed his MBBS from Karachi University and later pursued advanced surgical training, earning FCPS in Neurosurgery from the College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan (CPSP). His postgraduate credentials also include MCPS in General Surgery, reflecting a strong foundation in core surgical disciplines.

He has served at PIMS Hospital, Islamabad for 5 years, Fauji Foundation Hospital, Rawalpindi for 8 years and DHQ Hospital, Rawalpindi for 7 years, where he was actively involved in outpatient clinics, inpatient management, neurosurgical procedures, and emergency care. Also he remained the head of Holy Family Hospital, Rawalpindi. His clinical expertise covers a wide range of neurological and spinal conditions, including brain injuries, seizure disorders, loss of consciousness, hydrocephalus, spinal stenosis, and chronic backache.

In addition to clinical practice, Dr. Adil Aziz Khan has contributed to neurosurgical research with multiple peer-reviewed publications focusing on head injury, spinal surgery, intracranial pathology, and neurosurgical outcomes. He also has more than 10+ years of teaching experience
